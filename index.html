"African solutions to African problems"

Thabo Mbeki (South Africa president, 1999-2008)

Every year, Kenya loses up to sh. 150 Billion ($1.5 Billion) worth of agricultural produce through post-harvest losses. Up to 13% of these losses constitutes Irish potatoes, yet, if stored at between 5-10 Celsius and 93% relative humidity, Irish potatoes can be stored up to 5 months. Precision agriculture increases the yield and reduces post-harvest losses for smallholder farmers.

ClimateSense is a tool that senses temperature, humidity, soil moisture and soil nutrients, records and transmits the data within a local database and remotely through SMS & allows for the control of actuators such as water valves, fans, switches within a controlled environment
